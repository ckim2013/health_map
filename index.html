Prior to becoming a web developer, I worked in labs and hospitals. To stick to my roots and pay tribute to those afflicted with conditions, I created this health map using D3.js.


Obtaining the data for this project was particularly difficult. Some tables had different names for countries, different years of data, etc. This explains why some years may have little to no information. Regardless, this map should give a general idea of the disparities in health around the world.


Data was obtained from the WHO and the UN.
